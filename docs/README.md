## 🎯 System Overview

AgriGuru is a comprehensive AI-powered agricultural management platform designed to serve multiple stakeholders in the agricultural ecosystem:

### 👥 User Types
- **Farmers** - Crop management, market access
- **Agricultural Experts/Advisors** - Consultation, guidance
- **Suppliers/Vendors** - Equipment, seeds, fertilizers
- **Buyers** - Crop procurement
- **Government Officials** - Policy, subsidies
- **System Administrators** - Platform management

### 🌟 Core Modules
1. Crop Planning & Management
2. Weather Monitoring & Alerts
3. Pest & Disease Identification
4. Market Price Tracking
5. Expert Consultation Hub
6. Equipment Rental/Purchase Portal
7. Financial Services & Loans
8. Training & Education Center
9. Supply Chain Management
10. Quality Assessment Tools
11. Government Schemes Portal
12. Analytics & Reporting Dashboard

### 🔗 External Integrations
- Weather APIs (OpenWeatherMap, AccuWeather)
- Payment Gateways (Razorpay, PayPal, Stripe)
- SMS/WhatsApp Services
- Google Maps/GPS Services
- Image Recognition APIs
- Government Agriculture APIs
- Banking Services
- Email Services
- Cloud Storage (AWS S3)
